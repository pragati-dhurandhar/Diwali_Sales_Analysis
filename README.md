This project involves an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a Diwali sales dataset. The primary goal is to clean and analyze the data to understand customer purchasing patterns and identify key sales drivers. By exploring demographics (like age, gender, marital status, and state) and product-level trends, the analysis aims to uncover actionable insights for business growth.

## ‚ùì Key Questions & Objectives

This analysis seeks to answer several business-critical questions:

  * Which customer demographics (gender, age group, marital status) contribute most to sales revenue?
  * Which states are the top-performing in terms of orders and total sales amount?
  * What are the most popular product categories, and which specific products drive the most sales?
  * What is the relationship between customer age and their purchasing power?
  * What are the key conclusions and actionable insights that can be shared with the sales and marketing teams?
